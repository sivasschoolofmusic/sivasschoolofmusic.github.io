About Us

Sivas' School of Music was founded in 2010 with a vision to create a nurturing environment where students of all ages and abilities can discover their musical potential. For over 15 years, we have been committed to providing high-quality music education that balances technical proficiency with creative expression.

Our school is built on the belief that music education goes beyond learning notes and techniques; it's about developing discipline, boosting confidence, and fostering a lifelong appreciation for the arts. We take pride in our inclusive approach, welcoming students from diverse backgrounds and with varying musical interests.

What sets us apart is our team of dedicated instructors who are not only accomplished musicians but also passionate educators. They bring their real-world performance experience into the classroom, offering students valuable insights into the music industry while nurturing their individual talents.

Whether you're taking your first steps in music or looking to refine advanced skills, Sivas' School of Music provides the guidance, resources, and supportive community to help you achieve your musical goals.

Our Branches

Main City Center Branch

Located in the heart of downtown, our main branch offers state-of-the-art facilities including soundproofed practice rooms, recording studios, and a 150-seat recital hall. This location hosts our full range of programs, from early childhood music education to advanced instrumental and vocal training. With convenient access to public transportation and nearby parking, our city center location serves students from across the metropolitan area.

Westside Neighborhood Branch

Our Westside branch provides a welcoming, community-focused environment perfect for families and students of all ages. Established in 2018, this location features comfortable teaching spaces, a dedicated percussion room, and a cozy performance area for student recitals and community events. The Westside branch specializes in beginner and intermediate instruction, with a strong focus on youth programs and group classes that foster musical collaboration.

Our Teaching Philosophy

At Sivas' School of Music, we believe that effective music education combines technical mastery with creative expression and emotional connection. Our teaching philosophy is built on four core principles that guide all our programs.

First, we emphasize personalized learning, recognizing that each student has unique strengths, challenges, and goals. Our instructors create customized lesson plans that adapt to individual learning styles and evolve as students progress.

Second, we take a holistic approach to music education, incorporating theory, ear training, composition, and performance skills into our curriculum. We believe that understanding the "why" behind music is just as important as the "how."

Third, we foster a supportive community where students can learn from each other and grow through collaboration. Regular performance opportunities help students build confidence while developing their musical identity.

Finally, we instill a lifelong love of music by making learning enjoyable and relevant to each student's interests, whether they aspire to become professional musicians or simply wish to enrich their lives through music.

Our Certificates

Sivas' School of Music is proud to be an official examination center for Trinity College London, offering students the opportunity to earn internationally recognized music qualifications. These certifications provide a structured framework for progression while validating students' achievements at every stage of their musical journey.

Our Trinity-certified courses cover a wide range of instruments and vocal styles, from classical to contemporary. Students benefit from a comprehensive curriculum that develops technical proficiency, musicality, and performance skills. Examinations are conducted by external assessors from Trinity College, ensuring objective evaluation and maintaining high standards.

Beyond formal qualifications, we also offer our own internal certification programs designed to celebrate milestones and motivate continued learning. These certificates recognize not only technical achievements but also progress in areas such as musicianship, ensemble participation, and creative expression.

Whether pursuing music professionally or for personal enrichment, our certification options provide valuable credentials that are respected by educational institutions and employers worldwide. Our instructors guide students through preparation for these examinations, ensuring they approach assessment with confidence and achieve their full potential.
